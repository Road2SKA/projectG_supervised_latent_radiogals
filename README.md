# projectG_supervised_latent_radiogals

This is the repo for project G: **Latent space semantics for labelled and unlabelled radio galaxies**

Idea: apply a self-supervised classification

## Bibliography

#### Radio Data
 - [Horton et al. 2025](https://www.aanda.org/articles/aa/abs/2025/07/aa53559-24/aa53559-24.html): Human-made classification of LoTSS DR2 radio galaxies. ~10,000 samples.

#### DL techniques
 - [Grill et al. 2020](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.07733): Self-supervised representation learning with BYOL.
 - [Zeng et al. 2023](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2306.00112): BYOL guided by labels (search for BYOL-Sup in the paper).
 - [Khosla et al. 2020](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.11362): Supervised contrastive learning with SimCLR (positive pairs are sampled through data augmentation and label similarity).


#### Self-supervised learning on radio astronomy images

 - [Lochner and Rudnick 2025](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/ada14c): BYOL applied on MGCLS data.
 - [Perez et al. 2025](https://www.aanda.org/articles/aa/full_html/2025/07/aa54735-25/aa54735-25.html): SimCLR applied to LoTSS-DR2 data.
